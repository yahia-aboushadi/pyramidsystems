Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about AIR-Quire, federal acquisition modernization, and how to get started.

What is AIR-Quire?

AIR-Quire is an AI-powered federal acquisition platform developed by Pyramid Systems. It modernizes the entire procurement lifecycle — from requirements development through contract award and administration — replacing manual, disconnected processes with intelligent, automated workflows designed specifically for federal agencies.

What is the DoW Tradewinds Solutions Marketplace and why does it matter?

Tradewinds is the DoW Chief Digital and AI Office's (CDAO) marketplace for validated AI and digital solutions. AIR-Quire has earned Awardable status on Tradewinds, meaning it has been reviewed and approved for direct purchase by federal agencies — significantly reducing the time and effort required to procure and deploy the platform.

Which agencies can use AIR-Quire?

AIR-Quire is available to all federal agencies. It was purpose-built for the federal acquisition environment and is already being deployed at HUD through the Acquisition Management Support Services (AMSS) contract. Any agency looking to modernize its procurement process can access AIR-Quire through the DoW Tradewinds marketplace.

How long does it take to deploy AIR-Quire?

AIR-Quire is designed for rapid deployment. Because it is built on a low-code platform and pre-configured for federal acquisition workflows, agencies typically move from concept to production deployment in 12–20 weeks — significantly faster than traditional custom development. Contact us for a deployment timeline specific to your agency's environment.

Is AIR-Quire compliant with federal regulations?

Yes. AIR-Quire is built with federal compliance at its core — supporting FAR and DFAR requirements, maintaining full audit trails, and generating the documentation required for compliant acquisition decisions. Security and compliance are not afterthoughts — they are foundational to how the platform was designed.

Does AIR-Quire integrate with existing agency systems?

AIR-Quire is designed to integrate with existing federal IT ecosystems including procurement systems, financial management platforms, and document management tools. Our implementation team works with your agency to configure integrations that fit your current environment without disrupting ongoing operations.

What makes AIR-Quire different from other acquisition tools?

Most acquisition tools digitize existing paper processes — AIR-Quire reimagines them. By embedding AI throughout the acquisition lifecycle, AIR-Quire doesn't just speed up what you already do — it makes the entire process smarter. Agencies using AIR-Quire have reduced procurement lead time by 70% or more, cut GAO protest response timelines by 50%+, and generated FAR-required acquisition packages in days instead of months. Combined with Pyramid's 30 years of federal acquisition expertise, AIR-Quire is the only solution built by people who have lived the federal procurement process firsthand.

How do we get started with AIR-Quire?

Getting started is straightforward. Request a demo through this page and our team will schedule a tailored walkthrough of the platform for your agency. If you're ready to procure, AIR-Quire is available for direct purchase through the DoW CDAO Tradewinds Solutions Marketplace — no lengthy competitive procurement required.
