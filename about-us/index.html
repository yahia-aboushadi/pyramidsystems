Founder-led since 1995

The Pyramid Story

Sherry and Jeff Hwang built Pyramid Systems to pay forward the American dream. Three decades and one founding HUD contract later, that mission still shapes how every program we run is staffed, scoped, and shipped.

Sherry and Jeff Hwang moved to America with the dream of a better life. They met at Georgia State University, where both graduated with master's degrees in Computer Information Systems. In 1995, they teamed up to create Pyramid Systems. Their first client was the U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development. Hard work and dedication to that client earned Pyramid a sterling reputation at HUD, which opened doors at the federal agencies that still trust us today.

Sherry and Jeff are committed to delivering best-in-class technology solutions, providing superior customer service, and exceeding customer expectations. They have a passion and duty to strengthen America through technology and to pay forward the American dream for others. The four principles below are how that commitment shows up in the work.

Jeff Hwang

Principal, Co-Founder & Chairman of the Board

Mission before metrics

From day one, Pyramid measured success by whether the system served its mission — not by story points closed or hours billed. The principle still drives every program today.

Built to outlast contracts

Open-source tooling, federal-standard languages, IaC, and documentation written so a new prime can pick up the program without a rebuild. The discipline outlives any single award.

Same senior engineers, year one to year five

The architects who design the system are the engineers who operate it three years later. No bait-and-switch staffing, no parachute-in consultants. Continuity is the deliverable.

Compliance posture you can prove

SBOMs, SAST scans, control attestations, and audit evidence are emitted by the pipeline itself — not assembled in a scramble before a review. Whether the auditor is an ISSM, an OIG, or a SOC 2 examiner, the evidence is already there.

Sherry Hwang

Principal & Co-Founder
