Why people stay

What you get when you join Pyramid

Pyramid is employee-owned. Every team member shares in the company's success. These are the benefits we offer because we know the work we do depends on the people who show up to do it.

Employee Stock Ownership Plan. Every Pyramid employee is an owner. Real equity. Real skin in the game.

Comprehensive Health, Dental & Vision coverage from day one for you and your family.

401(k) with company match. Long-term thinking starts on day one.

Flexible PTO + every federal holiday. Take time when you need it.

Annual professional development funds: certifications, conferences, training, and continuing education.

Paid parental leave so you can be there when family needs you most.

What we look for

The kind of person who thrives here

We're not for everyone. Federal IT modernization is high-stakes, high-judgment work. Here's what we look for in the people who do it well.

You see federal IT as service, not just a paycheck. The work matters because the mission matters.

Certifications and skill growth are motivation, not obligation. The stack always moves forward.

Constructive feedback goes both ways for you. You give it well and you receive it well. Both are how teams grow.

You push solutions and shape direction. You don't wait for tickets to find you.

You make the team better. The mission is shared. The credit is shared.
