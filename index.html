Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about working with Pyramid Systems

How do we engage with Pyramid Systems?

Getting started is straightforward. Reach out via our contact page or call us directly at 703.553.0800. We'll schedule an initial conversation to understand your agency's needs, current challenges, and goals. From there, we'll identify the right engagement model — whether that's through an existing contract vehicle or a new procurement.

Which federal agencies do you serve?

Pyramid has delivered technology solutions across civilian and financial regulatory agencies since 1995. Our longest and deepest relationship is with the U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD), where we've supported mission-critical systems for decades. We also serve the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC), the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation (FDIC), and agencies under HHS including CMS, among others.

What contract vehicles are available?

We hold multiple government-wide and agency-specific contract vehicles to simplify procurement, including GSA IT Schedule 70, GSA OASIS+ Unrestricted, GSA 8(a) STARS III, HHS CMS SPARC, SEC ONE IT IDIQ, FDIC ITAS III Next Gen BOA, and the HUD O&M BPA. If you don't see your vehicle listed, contact us — we can often find a path to award.

Do you work with prime contractors and subcontractors?

Yes. Pyramid operates as both a prime contractor and a subcontractor depending on the opportunity. We have established teaming relationships across the federal IT ecosystem and welcome conversations with both agencies and industry partners looking to team on upcoming opportunities.

What makes Pyramid different from other IT contractors?

Three things set us apart. First, we've been exclusively focused on federal agencies for 30 years — we don't split our attention between commercial and government markets. Second, we build products, not just deliver services — AIR-Quire, our AI-powered acquisition platform, is live and Awardable on the DoW Tradewinds Solutions Marketplace. Third, as an employee-owned company, our people have a direct stake in the quality of every engagement.

What certifications does Pyramid hold?

Pyramid is CMMI Level 3 certified for both Development (CMMI-DEV) and Services (CMMI-SVC), ISO 9001:2015 certified, and an AWS Advanced Tier Partner with multiple AWS competencies. These certifications reflect our commitment to process excellence, quality management, and technical depth.

What is Pyramid Labs?

Pyramid Labs is our internal innovation engine — a dedicated team focused on rapid prototyping and deploying emerging technology solutions for mission use cases. Labs is where we experiment with AI, low-code platforms, and automation tools to develop production-ready capabilities faster than traditional development cycles allow. AIR-Quire was born out of Pyramid Labs.

What is AIR-Quire and where is it available?

AIR-Quire is Pyramid's AI-powered federal acquisition platform, designed to modernize and streamline the full government procurement lifecycle — from requirements development through award, execution, and contract closeout. It has earned Awardable status on the DoW CDAO Tradewinds Solutions Marketplace, making it available for direct purchase by federal agencies without a lengthy competitive procurement process. AIR-Quire is currently being deployed at HUD through the Acquisition Management Support Services (AMSS) program, and has been successfully evaluated by the U.S. Army Open Solicitation. Visit pyramidsystems.com to learn more or schedule a demo.
